Deuterium Tank
The Deuterium tank is for storing newly-synthesized deuterium. Once it is processed by the synthesizer, it is piped into this tank for later use. With each upgrade of the tank, the total storage capacity is increased. Once the capacity is reached, the Deuterium Synthesizer is shut down to prevent the tanks rupture.
